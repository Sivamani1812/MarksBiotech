LIVE PATIENTS 'WISDOM TEETH
EXTRACTION COURSE'
IN HANOI, VIETNAM

This course combines theoretical insights into third molar extraction management with hands-on experience on live patients. Participants will learn preoperative assessment and surgical techniques for the removal of varying complexity third molars, aiming to boost dentistsâ€™ confidence in this practice.

The course also provides an opportunity for participants to practice safe and efficient extraction techniques.

LECTURE & HANDS-ON:

- 1-day lecture provides extensive knowledge of preoperative assessment of third molars of varying complexity and the surgical techniques.

Work in pairs

- Perform min 12 cases on your own, and also assist and observe up to 12 cases surgeries.

State-of-the-Art Dental Hospital

- Take part in the program within a state-of-the-art dental hospital, ensuring a modern and specialised learning facility.

Provision of All Materials

- Benefit from all necessary materials and tools.

Comfortable Itinerary with a 5-star Hotel:

- Enjoy comfortable transportation services whole trips in Hanoi.
- Stay for 4 nights with accommodation provided, along with breakfasts, breaks, and lunches during the course.

Day 1
Lecture for anatomy, flap design, correct instrument handling, and extraction mechanics. Preview OPGs of live patients and conduct a surgical treatment planning session with the course instructor..

Day 2 & Day 3
Participants will extract wisdom teeth under close supervision from an experienced oral and maxillofacial surgeon, practicing correct surgical techniques in a clinical setting. The course aims to equip dentists with skills for performing wisdom tooth extractions of varying complexity, with dedicated time allocated to the removal of select cases.
